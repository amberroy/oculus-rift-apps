VR Snow Dodge

Web-VR Game with Oculus Rift DK2 positional tracking VRsnowdodge.com

PLAY NOW View on GitHub

VR Snow Dodge is a browser-based Virtual Reality (WebVR) game using the Oculus Rift DK2 positional tracking. Move your head to avoid snowballs thrown by the friendly snowperson!

How To Play

  • Move your body to dodge the snowballs coming at your head!
    Uses Oculus Rift DK2 positional tracking, no keyboard/mouse needed.
  • Press on-screen button or 'f' for fullscreen VR, 'Esc' to exit, 'z' to re-zero Rift.

Features

  • Snowball flight simulates real-world physics (with Physijs).
  • "One-to-one" tracking between player's real and in-game head position.
  • Sound effect when snowball hits the player's head or body.

Requirements

  1. Oculus Rift DK2 (order from Oculus)
  2. Desktop or Laptop computer running:
    • Oculus Runtime (download from Oculus)
    • VR-enabled build of Firefox Nightly (download from Mozilla)
      or Chromium (download from Google)
