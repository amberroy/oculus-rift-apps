VR Snow Dodge

VR Snow Dodge is a browser-based Virtual Reality (WebVR) game that uses the Oculus Rift DK2 positional head tracking. Move your head to avoid snowballs thrown by the snowman!

PLAY ONLINE. Requires Oculus Rift DK2 HMD and the latest VR-enabled build of Chromium or Firefox Nightly.

How To Play

  • Move your body to dodge the snowballs coming at your head.
    Uses Oculus Rift DK2 tracking camera, no keyboard/mouse needed.
  • Press on-screen button or 'f' for fullscreen VR, 'Esc' to exit, 'z' to re-zero Rift.

Features

  • Snowball flight simulates real-world physics (with Physijs).
  • "One-to-one" tracking between player's real and in-game head position.
  • Sound effect when snowball hits the player's head or body.
